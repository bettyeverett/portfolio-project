Catriona Mundle (RSA Cert.TEFLA, PGDip.CAA, Dip.BuqiTherapy)

Catriona has been a practicing astrologer since 1979. As an active member of the Astrological Lodge of London, she cofounded Regulus Publishing Co. to promote traditional astrology, while also studying contemporary psychological and counselling approaches. She holds a PG Diploma in Cultural Astronomy and Astrology, and enjoys teaching and giving talks. She is a Buqi Therapist and teaches Taijiwuxigong.
Her special interests include the Bipolar Temperament and using astrology to integrate nature and nurture.
